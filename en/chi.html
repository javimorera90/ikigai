Abdominal massage, harmony from the center

Our abdomen is much more than the center of digestion — it is where we process life, the engine of our strength and the refuge of our emotions. Often, stress manifests here as knots or tensions that restrict our breathing and freedom of movement.

In these sessions, we perform a deep yet gentle massage on the abdominal area to release accumulated burdens. I combine therapeutic touch with healing sounds drawn from Traditional Chinese Medicine, helping the organs recover their natural rhythm. By softening the center, we allow energy to flow toward the limbs, returning to the body its capacity to be the agile, balanced vehicle you need.

This massage can support you if you're looking for
Emotional and physical release
A space to let go of what we haven't been able to "digest", easing internal tensions and returning calm to your nervous system.
→
Space and breath
By freeing the diaphragm and abdominal area, you allow your breathing to become deeper and more efficient — essential for physical performance and presence.
→
Connection to your core
Strengthen awareness of your center, improving posture and the fluidity of movement from its deepest source.
→
Please note
This massage is performed fully clothed on a table. Coconut oil is used on the abdomen.
