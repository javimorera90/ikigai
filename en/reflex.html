Reflexology

Relief for the feet that ground us

Duration45 min
Fee50 €
Get in touch →

Our feet are the map of everything we are and the foundation of every movement that arises. They receive the impact, seek balance and connect us to the earth. In these sessions, we work through digital pressure on reflex zones to release accumulated burden and send a signal of calm throughout your body.

My approach seeks to return to your feet the sensitivity and structure they need to support you with lightness. Especially for those who inhabit movement and dance, these sessions offer a chance to restore your base — easing fatigue in the metatarsals and arch, and allowing tension to dissolve from the root.

How can it help you?
Grounding and restoration
A chance to come down from the head into the feet, releasing mental and physical stress built up after hours of training or standing.
→
Sensitivity and balance
By stimulating reflex zones, we improve proprioception and the responsiveness of your supports, fostering a more organic, stable and fluid balance.
→
Light feet for movement
Recover elasticity and space in your tissues so returning to the studio, the stage or daily life feels like a fresh start — with freer, more conscious steps.
→
Keep in mind
This massage is performed fully clothed on a table. Coconut oil is used on the feet.
