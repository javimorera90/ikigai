Thai massage

Movement to stretch your limits

Duration75 min
Fee62 €
Get in touch →

For me, this practice is a sacred dance between therapist and receiver. Thai massage takes place on a futon, in comfortable clothing, allowing us to explore your body's potential through deep stretches, pressure and rhythmic mobilizations. It's what we often call "assisted yoga".

My intention is to accompany your body as it recovers the spaces that stress or effort have gradually closed off. Working along the energy lines (Sen), we don't only unblock vital flow — we stretch muscle chains as a whole, preparing your body to be the vehicle that lets you release your full potential.

What can you expect from a session?
Recover your range
Free the joints and stretch deep musculature, giving your body an efficient, agile, pain-free range of movement.
→
Renewed energy
By mobilizing stagnant areas, that vitality becomes available again for your life — bringing physical lightness and a clearer mind.
→
Meditation in movement
An encounter to quiet the mind through rhythm and breath, allowing your nervous system to rest while your body expands.
→
Please note
This massage is performed fully clothed on a futon. No oils are used.
